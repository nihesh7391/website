About

More About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical Communication Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. My research interests are in the area of design and implementation of Cyber-Physical Systems and Internet of Things.

Howdy!

I was born and brought up in Valsad and Navsari, small cities of Gujarat, and where I spent my life before heading to a new city for graduation. Looking back now, it feels like the golden days of my life. I met some of my best friends there and made lifelong memories!
I left my hometown for pursuing my engineering dreams and moved to Nadiad for four years. A new city, college days, a few old friends, many new friends and adventures of a lifetime!
Then life took me to Hyderabad. A year full of uncertainty and a very hectic schedule. But, when you are accompanied by crazy friends, a few shenanigans are bound to happen!
Ultimately, I arrived at my current home. A place that I fell in love with and a city I have no plans of leaving. Indian Institute of Science and city of Bangalore.
I will share many of these experience in my blog, though in not in chronological order. I hope that you have as much fun while reading them as I had remembering them while writing them down for you.
